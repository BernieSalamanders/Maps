The Gardens Trail

Located between 130-138 Astor Drive in Rhinebeck, NY.

The Gardens Trail easement was obtained by Winnakee Land Trust in January of 2005 and has since been established as a public access trail. This full, half mile loop is ideal for hikers of all skill levels . Stop by for a quick hike and be sure to look for the parking lot. Winnakee will be establishing a trail kiosk at the site in 2016.

Thank you to Boy Scout Troop 128 for helping us prepare the trail for winter!
